About AuraTravel

AuraTravel originally started as TripLink, and has since evolved into an advanced, modern AI-powered travel booking platform connecting you with numerous operators across flights, buses, and trains globally.

We pride ourselves on offering a beautifully simple yet extraordinarily powerful routing engine that compares prices, transit durations, and operator statuses to bring you the best possible travel journey.
